28th International Film Festival of Kerala

  • Karthik B

Here are some of the awards from the 28th International Film Festival of Kerala (IFFK) in 2023:
Suvarna Chakoram for Best Film: Evil Does Not Exist by Ryusuke Hamaguchi
Rajata Chakoram for Best Director: Sunday by Shokir Kholikov
Rajata Chakoram for Best Debut Director: The Sentence/Thadavu by Fazil Razak
Audience Poll Award: The Sentence/Thadavu by Fazil Razak
FIPRESCI Award for Best International Film: Prison in the Andes by Felipe Carmona
NETPAC Award for Best Asian Film: Sunday by Shokir Kholikov
FFSI KR Mohanan Award for Best Debut Director from India: Kherwal by Uttam Kamati
NETPAC Award for the Best Malayalam Film: The Play (Aattam)
Lifetime Achievement Award: Christoph Sanusi
