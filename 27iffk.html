27th International Film Festival of Kerala

  • Karthik B

Here are some of the winners from the 27th International Film Festival of Kerala (IFFK) in 2022:
Best Film: Utama by Alejandro Loayza Grisi
Best Malayalam Film: Ariyippu by Mahesh Narayanan
Best Asian Film: Alam by Firas Khoury
Best Director: Tayfun Pirselimoglu for Kerr
Best Debut Director: Siddharth Chauhan for Amar Colony
Lifetime Achievement Award: Bela Tarr
Audience Poll Award: Nanpakal Nerathu Mayakkam by Lijo Jose Pellissery
Best International Film: Our Home by Romi Meitei
Best Malayalam Film by FIPRESCI: 19 1(a) by Indhu V.S.
Special Mention for Performance: Manisha Soni and Muskan for A Place of Our Own
The 2022 IFFK was held from December 9–16, and Deepika Suseelan took over as artistic director from Bina Paul.

My personal favorite : Triangle of Sadness(2022)
